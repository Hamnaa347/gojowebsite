Legendary Powers & Abilities

Gojo Satoru isn’t just any sorcerer — he’s the strongest, with abilities that seem almost divine.

  • Limitless Technique: Manipulates space to control attacks and defenses.
  • Six Eyes: Allows near-perfect perception of cursed energy and its flow.
  • Infinity: Creates an infinite space between himself and any threat.
  • Domain Expansion — Infinite Void: Overwhelms enemies with endless information, paralyzing them.

FAQ

  • What is Infinity?

    Infinity means limitless space between himself and others, making him untouchable through his cursed technique, Limitless.

  • What is Hollow Purple?

    Hollow Purple is Gojo Satoru’s devastating technique combining Blue and Red, erasing everything in its path with immense destructive power.

  • What is Domain Expansion?

    Gojo Satoru’s Domain Expansion, Unlimited Void, traps opponents in infinite information, overwhelming their senses and rendering them helpless within his control.
