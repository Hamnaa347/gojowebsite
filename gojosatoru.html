Gojo Satoru

Learn More Gojo pg.3

Gojo Satoru is a powerful sorcerer from Jujutsu Kaisen. He's stylish, sarcastic, and one of the strongest characters in anime.

Cool Facts About Gojo

  • Born December 7th, 1989
  • Wears a blindfold or sunglasses
  • Loves sweets like mochi
  • Can use "Domain Expansion: Infinite Void"

Quotes

"Throughout heaven and earth, I alone am the honored one."

"I'm the strongest, after all."
